  • Here are some FAQ which may help you in getting started.
    • How to get started?
    • On the home page, in top right corner, there are options of signup and sign in.Click on signup if you are a new user.
      After signing in you will be redirected to a page where you need to submit your address details.
      There you need to provide your address, some details about your self, whether you are signing as a user or a barber and most important you need to submit your coordinates(latitude and longitude) which you may not be knowing.
      To make this process easier, we have added a button, labelled as 'Access my location', on the top of the form where you have to submit your address details which on click auto-fills latitude, longitude and address fields.
    • How to fix an appointment?
    • After signing in, on the home page, in the top right corner, below the navigation bar, there is option icon.Click on that, choose a barber by clicking on his/her username then click on "Fix appointment" button.
      Option >> Barber >> Fix appointment.
      After that you will be taken on another page where you can select a time for appointment.Note that you should use a valid time else it is going to show an error.
    • What is a valid time?
    • You cannot select a past time or acccurate present time.By using such times, you will see an error showing that "You cannot fix appointment for a past time."
      Our app keeps a 15 minute gap between two consecutive appointments.So if you are getting some error like "Sorry! You can not fix appointment for the time selected.Please change the time(Try increasing or decreasing the time by 15 to 30 minutes.).", then do the same i.e, try increasing or decreasing the time by 15-30 minutes to get an available time.
      Another possibility is that if you are getting an error saying "Sorry! You can not fix appointment for the time selected.Please change the time." This error is shown only when you are trying to fix an appointment at a time which is already taken. The number of people who can fix appointment at a particular time is equal to the number of employees working under the selected barber. So again the solution is changing the time by 15-30 minutes.
    • How to check my appointments?
    • Follow the given path;
      In the top right corner;
      username >> My appointments
      You can do a lot of things there.You can mark the appointment as completed or cancel it.
      By clicking on the username, you will also see a lot of options like "Change E-mail", where you can modify your email list linked with our site,
      "Change Password" where you can change password.You will get an email where you will see a link.By clicking on that link, you can change password.
      "Profile" where you can see and update your profile.You can also delete your account permanently there.
      "Sign out" where you can sign out of the site.
